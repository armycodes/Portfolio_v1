# One More Thing (Most Important)

Bro, **don't think of this as a portfolio.**

Think of it as a **book**.

A good book has:

-   A strong cover (Hero)
-   An introduction (About)
-   Chapters (Projects)
-   Marginal notes (Section quotes)
-   A final page that stays with the reader (Contact)

If we design it with that mindset, people won't just scroll---they'll
**read** it.

And that's where your writer side becomes a genuine advantage.
